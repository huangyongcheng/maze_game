# 🏠 Home to Cisbox 🏢

> *A cute maze game — find your way from home to the office*

---

## 🎮 What is this game?

**Home to Cisbox** is a mini maze game where you control a tiny cute character traveling from **home** 🏠 to the **Cisbox office** 🏢. Sounds simple, but... you don't get to see the whole map! 👀

The game is designed in a cute style with soft pastel colors — perfect for a short break during the workday.

---

## 🎯 Goal

Travel from the starting point (🏠 Home) to the destination (🏢 Cisbox) in the **shortest time possible**.

The game tracks your **personal best** for each difficulty — challenge yourself to break your own record!

---

## 🕹️ How to play

### Step 1: Pick a difficulty
When you open the app, you'll see 3 options:

| Difficulty | Maze size | Visibility | Best for |
|---|---|---|---|
| ⭐ **Easy** | 10 × 10 | See 3 cells away | Newcomers |
| ⭐⭐ **Medium** | 15 × 15 | See 2 cells away | Familiar players |
| ⭐⭐⭐ **Hard** | 20 × 20 | Only adjacent cells | A real challenge |

### Step 2: Control the character

There are **2 ways** to control (use whichever you prefer):

**🎮 Option 1 — D-pad (on-screen buttons):**
- 4 arrow buttons at the bottom of the screen
- Press ▲ to move up, ▼ down, ◄ left, ► right

**👆 Option 2 — Swipe the screen:**
- Swipe directly on the maze in the direction you want to move
- Faster and more natural

> 💡 *In Settings, you can enable either one or both at the same time.*

### Step 3: Find your way to Cisbox!

- The character can only see **a small area around them** — the rest is hidden by **fog** 🌫️
- You need to explore to discover the path
- Bumping into a wall makes the character "bounce" back — you can't walk through walls
- Each step plays a gentle footstep sound 👣

---

## ⏱️ Time system

The timer starts as soon as you enter the game. It's shown in the **top bar**.

### 🆘 "Reveal Map" feature

If you're really stuck, you can tap the 👁️ icon to **reveal the entire map**.

**But there's a price:**
- Each time you turn "Reveal Map" ON → **+30 seconds** added to your total time ⏰
- No limit on how many times you can toggle, but the more you reveal, the worse your time
- You can turn it on and off freely, but every time you switch it **ON**, the penalty applies

> 🎯 *Tip: Only reveal when you really need to. Skilled players try to finish without revealing the map at all.*

---

## ⏸️ Pause / Resume

- Tap the **⏸ Pause** button in the top bar any time
- The timer stops — paused time doesn't count
- Tap **Resume** to keep playing, or **Quit** to return to the main menu

---

## 🏆 Best-time system

- The game automatically saves your **best time** per difficulty
- Stored locally on your device (no network, no account needed)
- When you finish a run, the win screen shows:
  - ⏱️ Your time for this run
  - 🏆 The current best time (or "New Record!" if you beat it)

## 💡 Tips & tricks

### 🎓 Beginners
1. **Start with Easy** — get used to the controls and the game's feel
2. **Move slowly** — speed isn't everything; finding the right path matters more
3. **Remember junctions** — if you hit a dead end, back up and try another direction

### 🎯 Intermediate
1. **Avoid "Reveal Map"** if you can — the 30-second penalty is heavy
2. **Use the right-hand rule**: always turn right when possible — a classic maze-solving trick that's guaranteed to find the exit
3. **Watch the fog edges carefully** — sometimes you can guess the path shape from what's barely visible

### 🔥 Pro
1. **Try a "no-reveal run"** — finish Hard without ever revealing the map
2. **Speedrun** — beat your personal best on every difficulty
3. **Learn the pattern**: the maze generator uses Recursive Backtracking → corridors tend to have a similar "vibe"

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Is the maze always solvable?**
A: Yes! The game uses the *Recursive Backtracking* algorithm to generate a "perfect maze" — there is always **exactly one path** from Home to Cisbox.

**Q: Is each maze different?**
A: Completely different! Every time you start a run, a brand-new random maze is generated.

**Q: What if I quit mid-game?**
A: The game doesn't save in-progress runs — quitting loses your progress. But your existing best time is preserved.

**Q: Can I play offline?**
A: Fully offline. No internet, no login required.

**Q: Is there any way to cheat?**
A: 😏 You can reveal the map non-stop, but your time will be terrible. The real glory is finishing Hard with a great time and zero reveals!
